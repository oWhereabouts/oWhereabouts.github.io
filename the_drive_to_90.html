The Drive to 90, COVID-19 Vaccination Uptake in New Zealand

This map was made a leaving gift for a team member when I was on secondment at the Ministry of Health in the Vaccination Program during COVID-19. They had a background in spatial and had done incredible work in the team so wanted to create a map with impact that would be a reminder of what the work they did helped accompish which they would be proud to hang on their wall. This map has very little supporting information, such as annotations of the trends seen, as they had such a deep understanding of the data this seemed unnecessary.
