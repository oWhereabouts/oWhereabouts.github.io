Unite Against COVID Webmap

Whilst it was Eagle Technologies who produced the webmap I was responsible for providing and updating the data for this webmap. This involved writing a script to standup a python virtual environment with The Arcgis API so geojson could be used to update layers in Arcgis Online. This map had a significant impact in driving the uptake of COVID-19 vaccination by allow the public to see and compare vaccination uptake in their area. It had over 1 million hits per day at times.
